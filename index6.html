Grandma's Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add milk, oil, eggs, and vanilla. Beat until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into a greased baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let the cake cool before serving. Enjoy!
